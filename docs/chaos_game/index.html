The Chaos Game!

Here are the rules for this:

  1. Step 1: 3 random points are randomly picked anywhere on the screen.
  2. Step 2: A random point is picked completely randomly on the screen.
  3. Step 3: The random point then randomly chooses any of the 3 points and then a new point is added halfway through it.
